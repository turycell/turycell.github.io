  • Hello, fellow judge, willing to call a Magic draft!

    All you have to do is read the text on the screen and watch the players.

    The program is controlled with a single button on the screen. Simply press a space button on your keyboard to advance.

    Magic drafting application version 2.4.  2016. About. Homepage.

    Sets:

  • Welcome to the draft.

    We will draft three boosters — SET, SET and SET.

    Please make sure you have these three packs in front of you.

    During the draft, when I say ‘Look’, you may look at the cards in your hand. I will remind you, when the time is nearly up and when I say ‘Pass’, you should pass your cards at once. If possible, shuffle the cards and lay them out on the table in packs of 3.

    When your neighbour has passed his cards to you, pick them up, but don't look at them until I say so.

  • Between the boosters, you will have some time to review the cards you have drafted that far.

    During the draft procedure you should stay focused on your cards and try not to give away any kind of information to your opponents.

  • Please open the first booster, which is SET, and count the cards face-down. There should be 14 cards in there.

  • You have to pass the cards now to your left.

    Prepare for the draft and stay silent.

  • You now have 30 seconds to review what you have drafted.

    30

    10 seconds!

    Put back the cards. Keep them in one pile.

  • Please open the second booster, which is SET, and count the cards face-down. There should be 14 cards in there.

    You have to pass the cards now to your right.

  • You now have 45 seconds to review what you have drafted.

    45

    10 seconds!

    Put back the cards. Keep them in one pile.

  • Please open the third booster, which is SET, and count the cards face-down. There should be 14 cards in there.

    You have to pass the cards now to your left.

  • The draft is now over.

    Please take your cards and proceed to your sitting place. Try to avoid giving information related to the draft to other players.

    Offline use

    To save this application and use it offline, use your browser’s ‘File → Save Page’ function.

    iPhone/iPad

    On iOS you can ‘add to homepage’ the application and it will be available offline.
